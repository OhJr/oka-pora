Community-based Tourism: Experiencing Shanenawa Culture

The Shanenawa community has been developing inspiring sustainable tourism initiatives, inviting visitors to experience their traditions and way of life up close. More than just a trip, it is a cultural and spiritual immersion guided by respect for nature and indigenous wisdom.

During their stay, visitors have the opportunity to take part in village activities, learn about the use of medicinal plants, listen to ancestral stories, and understand the deep bond the Shanenawa people maintain with the forest. This exchange of knowledge reinforces the importance of environmental preservation and the appreciation of native cultures.

These initiatives are a powerful example of sustainability and community leadership, showing how tourism can strengthen culture and generate income without neglecting care for the Earth.
