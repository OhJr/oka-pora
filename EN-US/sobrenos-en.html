Who We Are - Oka-Porã

We are a project/organization that creates content, organizes expeditions, and connects people interested in culture, nature, and technology.

Our History

Founded in 2025, Oka-Porã was born from the desire to explore places with indigenous themes and showcase the natural and cultural beauty of Brazil. Since then, we have carried out several expeditions, published countless articles, and established valuable partnerships.

Our Team

  • João Camargo — Founder and Executive Director. Explorer passionate about Indigenous cultures and environmental conservation.
  • Ananda Nardi — Content Coordinator. Journalist specialized in environmental and cultural topics.
  • Bruno Camargo — Expedition Leader. Experienced guide with extensive background in trails and outdoor adventures.
  • Ana Maia — Partnerships Manager. Specialist in developing strategic partnerships and collaborations.
  • Osvaldo Costa — Graphic Designer. Responsible for the visual identity and graphic materials of the project.

What We Do

We offer a variety of services and projects, including a blog with informative articles, guided expeditions, detailed guides, interactive workshops, and strategic partnerships.
